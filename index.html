About us

Isaiah Oluwafemi Odufala
Managing Director

Mr. Femi Odufala graduated from Regent University College of Science and Technology with a bachelor's degree in control and instrumentation engineering. He also has an HND in Electrical Engineering from Ilorin's Kwara State Polytechnic. He is a Health and Safety Officer, as well as a Project Manager, and a member of the America Society for Quality (ASQ). He has been... actively involved in the growth of several start-up firms into very profitable going operations over the years as a seasoned entrepreneur. He worked for Anchor Telecoms Ghana as a Health, Safety, Environment, and Quality (HSEQ) Manager, where he served Energetically and ensured that accidents were avoided by ongoing training of staff on site. He was the Head of Operations at Northward Techniques Ghana Limited and the Operations Manager at Richard Brainsworth Limited, where he was responsible for a variety of initiatives. He also worked for Phemmith Engineering Company as an Electrical Supervisor and Power Holding Company of Nigeria as a System Operator. His broad engineering background has provided him an advantage in every project he has worked on. Construction of telecommunication masts, fabrication of mast antennae poles (obliques), fiber installation; outside plant (OSP) & aerial, technical equipment installations, industrial and home electrical installations, and so on are some of the projects. He has overseen many Fibre projects in Ghana, as well as managing day-to-day operations of workers on-site, directing and coordinating activities of businesses or departments concerned with product production, pricing, sales, or distribution, directing and coordinating the organization's financial and budget activities to fund operations, maximize investments, and increase efficiency, and overseeing the maintenance and repair of machinery, equipment, and electrical and mechanical systems.

Jonas Korto
Admin & Finance Director

Jonas is currently the Finance and Administrative Manager of Esaiert Risorsa. Jonas has brought on board and in-depth experience in Finance, Accounting, and Administration to assist the company to achieve its objectives. Jonas worked with various organizations in versatile roles in Accounting, Finance, Audit, and Administration. Jonas currently... provides consultancy services to his clients in Finance, Accounting, and Administration. Before this role, Jonas was the Head of Finance of Newco Catering and Logistics Limited (an oil and gas catering company). He also worked with Strategic Communications Africa Limited (Stratcomm Africa) as Principal Finance Officer and later acted in the capacity of Head of Finance. He has equally worked with Newrest Ghana and Newrest Logistics Limited (a multinational airline catering company) where he held the position of Assistant Finance Manager, reporting on the performance and position of the company to management and related roles. Jonas Yao Korto is a Chartered Accountant and a member of both Institute of Chartered Accountants, Ghana (ICAG) and the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA, UK) He has over Nineteen (19) years of working experience in Accounting, Auditing, and Financial and Administrative Management. Jonas also holds a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Psychology and Philosophy from the University of Ghana Legon.

Dr. Moses Akazue
ICT Director

Dr Moses Akazue leads the design, deployment and maintenance of technology interventions at Esaiert Risorsa. Dr Akazue is a seasoned Computer Scientist and IT practitioner with a BSc in Computer Science, MSc in Advanced Computer Science, and a PhD in... Human-Computer Interaction. He has over 12 years experience designing technology solutions for real problems posed by real users. As an expert in designing high impact user-centred IT solutions, Dr Akazue has extensive experience in tailoring automation to meet the needs of users. Dr Akazue is also highly skilled in stakeholder management and empirically evaluating the impact of new techniques and technology interventions using a blend of qualitative and quantitative methods.
